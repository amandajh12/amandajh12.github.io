Music

Everyone likes music, to no surprise, which is why I didn't think much of me being obsessed with different pop stars and watching musical tv shows growing up. But once I found my way to the radio station on campus, I knew this had to mean a little more. My freshmen year I was torn between where to invest my time; I had just joined the training class for KUCI but I was also involved in other clubs that I realized I just didn't care about anymore. At this time, in that magical December of 2016, I listened to Childish Gambino's Awaken! My Love. By the time I got to Stand Tall and I heard the chorus... "Keep all your dreams, keep standing tall...", it was over. It hit me. I went all in and finished my demo tape and got a radio show called Belly of the Beat on KUCI 88.9 FM in Irvine. From there, I've maintained my show for about a year now. I've sound engineered for bands that perform live in our station, and this past fall, I helped sound engineer for an event for the first time. I'm also Assistant Marketing Director and my goal is to help to bring more cool events and build up the KUCI brand as the underground pirate radio station of the Orange County area that it has been for over 40 years.
